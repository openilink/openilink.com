## Why OpenILink

OpenILink is the public face of a practical stack: lean SDKs, stable integration patterns, and docs that stay close to actual implementation. The goal is straightforward. Teams should be able to discover the platform, understand the mental model, and start shipping without reverse-engineering examples from scattered repos.
